---
title: Requirements
---

The bar to running your own Samourai Dojo is not high and is accessible to almost anyone that can acquire an entry level PC with a reliable internet connection and keep the device on 24/7.

Whether you choose to use a laptop, desktop or mini-PC is up to your personal preference and they have their own trade-offs with respect to price, form factor, power supply options, the need for external ancillaries as well as the ability to upgrade.

It is not necessary to use a brand new computer. Refurbished or renewed machines manufactured within the last five years with the attributes below will perform far better than the newest single board computers.

## Specifications
- x86-64/ARM architecture
- 2 GHz dual-core or better processor
- Minimum 8 GB RAM
- 2TB NVMe based SSD

## Recommended software
- Debian based operating system such as Ubuntu 24.04 LTS.

## Recommended hardware
- Devices such as HP EliteDesk / EliteBook, Dell OptiPlex, Lenovo ThinkCenter / ThinkPad and the Intel NUC, RockPro64, Rock5b range.
